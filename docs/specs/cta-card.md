## C. Professional Individual

Person reviewing paperwork at desk.

Use for:

- High income audience
- Practical / rational buyers

---

# 7. Image Style Rules

Need:

- Realistic photography
- Age 28–40
- Warm natural light
- Authentic emotion
- Clean environment
- Upscale but relatable

Avoid:

- Fake AI hands
- Stock-photo smiles
- Overly staged poses
- Cluttered rooms
- Cheap-looking interiors
