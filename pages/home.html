Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are living benefits?
Living benefits are riders or built-in features that allow you to access a portion of your death benefit while you're still alive if you're diagnosed with a qualifying critical, chronic, or terminal illness.
Do I need a medical exam?
Many policies offer simplified or no-exam options. The requirement depends on your age, health, and the coverage amount you're applying for. We'll help you find the best fit.
How fast can I get covered?
Some policies can be approved in as little as 24–48 hours. Coverage timelines vary by carrier and the underwriting process required for your application.
How much does it cost?
Premiums depend on your age, health, coverage amount, and term length. Many families are surprised by how affordable coverage can be — especially when obtained at a younger age.
Who can apply?
Most adults between 18 and 70 can apply. Eligibility varies by carrier and health profile. Our licensed advisors will help match you with the right carrier for your situation.
